Matchday 3: Derby Drama in Mbekweni!

26 October 2024 | Mbekweni, Paarl

Matchday 3 saw us take on Mbekweni Cosmos in a heated local derby, filled with passion, intensity, and end-to-end action.

After a tough first half, we went into the break trailing, but the team came out fighting in the second half. Our persistence paid off when Sisekho found the equalizer, bringing us level and igniting our hopes of taking something from the game.

Just as it seemed we would walk away with a valuable point, Cosmos managed to find a heartbreaking late winner, sealing a narrow 2-1 defeat for us in the dying minutes.

Though the result didn’t go our way, the team showed resilience and determination—qualities that will drive us forward as we continue our ABC Motsepe League journey. We take the lessons, regroup, and come back stronger!

Matchday 2: Historic Debut in the ABC Motsepe League!

19 October 2024 | Hermanus

Our team made history as we played our first-ever ABC Motsepe League match against Salim United FC in Hermanus. Originally scheduled for Matchday 1, our debut had to be postponed to a later date, making this Matchday 2 clash even more special.

It was a hard-fought encounter, with both teams displaying resilience and determination, ending in a goalless draw (0-0). Despite the lack of goals, we showed great discipline, teamwork, and fighting spirit—proving that we belong at this level.

A solid defensive performance kept our opponents at bay, while our attacking unit created several promising chances. This result lays a strong foundation for the season ahead, and we are ready to keep pushing forward.
