Matchday 15: Hard-Fought Draw on the Road!

8 March 2025 | Plettenberg Bay, WC

A tough away battle against Oriented Birds FC, currently second on the log, ended in a 0-0 draw. Both teams played with great intensity, but neither could break the deadlock. Our defense stood strong, and we created a few chances, but the game remained evenly matched throughout. It was a hard-earned point against strong opposition, and the team showed resilience in a difficult away environment. We'll take the positives from this performance as we continue to fight for every point in the ABC Motsepe League.

Matchday 14: Derby Day Delight – Another Win at Home!

1 March 2025 | Mbekweni, Paarl

In the second derby of the season, we secured a 2-0 victory over Mbekweni Cosmos FC in front of our home crowd, and it was a performance to remember! Mustapha opened the scoring with a fantastic header goal, and just as Cosmos started to press, Charles sealed the win with a clinical finish to ensure all three points stayed in Mbekweni. The team showed great composure and determination, keeping a clean sheet and dominating throughout the match. A big thank you to our fans for creating an electrifying atmosphere that pushed us to victory!

Matchday 18: Solid Home Win – Aphiwe's Brace Secures Victory!

29 March 2025 | Mbekweni, Paarl

We bounced back with a 2-0 victory at home against Infantry School FC, thanks to a clinical brace from Aphiwe in the second half! The game was tightly contested in the first half, with both teams giving their all, but it was Aphiwe who made the difference after the break, finding the back of the net twice to seal the win and give us a much-needed three points. A fantastic performance all around, and a big thank you to our supporters for their incredible energy and backing as always!

Matchday 17: Tough Away Defeat

22 March 2025 | Beaufort West, WC

Unfortunately, we suffered a 1-0 defeat away to Ubuntu Spartans FC in a hard-fought match. The game was tight, but a penalty early in the second half was enough to seal the win for the opposition. Despite putting in a solid performance and creating a few chances, we couldn’t find the equalizer. It’s a disappointing result, but we’ll regroup, learn from this experience, and come back stronger in the next match.

Matchday 16: Tough Home Defeat

15 March 2025 | Mbekweni, Paarl

A tough day at the office as we fell 1-3 at home to Seaside Spur FC despite a strong start. We took an early 1-0 lead with a great goal from Rashidi in the opening minutes of the first half. However, the opposition responded quickly, equalizing just moments later, and then things took a turn as Seaside Spur added two more goals, leaving us 1-3 down at halftime. We tried to regroup in the second half, but the damage was done, and we couldn’t find a way back into the game. It was a disappointing result, but we’ll learn from it and bounce back stronger.
