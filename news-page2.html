Matchday 15: Hard-Fought Draw on the Road!

8 March 2025 | Plettenberg Bay, WC

A tough away battle against Oriented Birds FC, currently second on the log, ended in a 0-0 draw. Both teams played with great intensity, but neither could break the deadlock. Our defense stood strong, and we created a few chances, but the game remained evenly matched throughout. It was a hard-earned point against strong opposition, and the team showed resilience in a difficult away environment. We'll take the positives from this performance as we continue to fight for every point in the ABC Motsepe League.

Matchday 14: Derby Day Delight – Another Win at Home!

1 March 2025 | Mbekweni, Paarl

In the second derby of the season, we secured a 2-0 victory over Mbekweni Cosmos FC in front of our home crowd, and it was a performance to remember! Mustapha opened the scoring with a fantastic header goal, and just as Cosmos started to press, Charles sealed the win with a clinical finish to ensure all three points stayed in Mbekweni. The team showed great composure and determination, keeping a clean sheet and dominating throughout the match. A big thank you to our fans for creating an electrifying atmosphere that pushed us to victory!

Matchday 13: Important Return Leg Victory!

15 February 2025 | Mbekweni, Paarl

In the return leg against Salim United FC, we secured a 2-1 victory at home, continuing our strong run in the ABC Motsepe League! It was a closely fought battle, with both teams giving it their all. We showed great resilience to come from behind and claim all three points. The squad's determination and fighting spirit were on full display, and we made sure to make the most of our home advantage. Another fantastic result as we continue to grow and build momentum for the rest of the season.

Matchday 12: Last-Minute Magic Seals Another Away Win!

8 February 2025 | Beaufort West, WC

A tough away trip to Barcelona FC ended in dramatic fashion as we secured a 0-1 victory, thanks to a stunning last-minute free kick from Caleb!

The match was a hard-fought battle, with both teams creating chances but unable to find the breakthrough. As the clock ticked down, it looked like we were heading for a draw—until Caleb stepped up in the dying minutes to deliver a perfectly placed free kick and seal all three points! A massive win on the road and another statement performance from the squad. Huge credit to the team for fighting until the very end, and a special shoutout to our traveling supporters who backed us all the way!

Matchday 11: Battling Performance but Narrow Defeat

1 February 2025 | Mbekweni, Paarl

A tough encounter against log leaders Hanover Park FC saw us fall to a 1-2 defeat at home despite a strong performance.

The visitors struck first, taking an early lead, but we responded well, showing great character to find an equalizer before halftime and go into the break at 1-1. The second half remained tightly contested, but Hanover Park managed to capitalize on a key moment to score their second goal, which ultimately decided the match. Despite the loss, the team gave a spirited performance against top opposition, proving that we can compete with the best. We take the lessons, regroup, and come back stronger!
